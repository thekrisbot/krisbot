Welcome to Heavenos 2.0

St.Peter vs. the state of enterprise software...
March 19, 2026

CHARACTERS:


PETER — Saint Peter. Two thousand years at the gate. Never once lost a soul. Currently on page four of a seventeen page quick-start guide to Heaven OS 2.0. I

JASON — Early 20s. Golden retriever soul in a human body. Dead on Arrival.

Setting: Heaven. The onboarding suite. A large screen. A tablet on a stand. A motivational banner that reads “WELCOME TO YOUR ETERNAL JOURNEY”.

(Lights up. Peter stands at the tablet, laminated quick-start guide in one hand, squinting at it. He looks up. Jason is standing in front of him, mouth open, staring at a cloud.)

PETER: Right. Okay. Welcome. I'm Peter. Saint Peter. You're -- (checks guide) -- Jason Theodore Sal-zy... Salzy-cow... (quietly, to himself) Sal-zy-kow-ski.

(Jason, still looking at the cloud, slowly raises a finger to help.)

JASON: (to the cloud, quietly) Yo. What IS this place!?

PETER: Uh, this would be heaven.

JASON: (turning around) That's dope.

(Beat. Something lands.)

JASON: Wait. Does that mean I am dead!? (just realising)

PETER: That's the idea, yes.

JASON: But how?

PETER: Uh you have a friend named.. Let me see if i have this right.. Gatorade...Doug?

JASON: (nodding slowly) my homeboy killed me? 

PETER: Huh? What? No.. it says here that you lost a bet.

JASON: And loser has to die? 

PETER: (confused) No..You bet your friend Gatorade..

JASON: Doug.

PETER: Yeah Gatorade Doug.. that you can jump the turnstile at the Piccadilly station and slipped. Cracked your head open. 

JASON: That blows, dawg!

PETER: But hey! That's alright. Because you were such a kind person in your time on earth, you are now are in the happiest place known to human beings!

JASON: (lighting up) Wait. We are in Taco Bell?

PETER: What? Why would you... no.. this is heaven. You can get anything and everything you've wanted!

JASON: Dope! Can I get a Taco?

PETER: Uh sure... yes. But look around Jason. This place is your ultimate reward for everything you ever did on earth! Use your imagination!

JASON: Okay. Can I get two Tacos??

PETER: (resigned) Sure. As soon as we get through your paperwork, we can explore. (taps screen. A loading animation appears.) Oh -- there we go. It's loading.

(Jason already wandering toward cloud.)

PETER: Could you -- just stay -- (gestures at the spot in front of the tablet) -- just here. Just for now.

JASON: (drifting back, eyes still on cloud) Yeah yeah. What is this?

PETER: This is the -- (reading guide) -- the new HeavenOS 2.0. Our brand new onboarding interface. We've just migrated to it.

JASON: Cool. What did you use before then? 

PETER: (quietly) Gates. A Ledger. I'd check your name, you'd sign, done. 

(Loading finishes. A cheerful jingle plays. A video begins -- an angel in a fleece vest) 

HeavenOS 2.0:  Hi! I'm Clarence! Welcome to HeavenOS 2.0. Let's get you set up for eternity! First--

(Buffering. The video freezes on Clarence's face.)

PETER: (reaching for skip) New software. I guess we can just skip this for -- (the skip button is greyed out) -- nope. You cannot skip.

JASON: (watching the frozen screen, interested) Who's Clarence?

PETER: One of my team members. She is part of the implementation team.

(Video resumes suddenly. Starts and stops. Stuttering playback.)

ONBOARDING VIDEO: -- and once you've completed the sign up process you'll receive your personalised eternity profile, your welcome halo, and access to the full suite of heavenly amen--

(Buffering again. Frozen again.)

(Jason has drifted back toward the cloud.)

PETER: Jason.

JASON: It's so soft, Pete. Come take a look!

PETER: I know. Please -- can we focus?

(Peter finds the right page on the guide. Taps the screen. A form appears.)

PETER: Okay. Full name.

JASON: Jason.

PETER: FULL name.

JASON: Jason Theodore Salzycowski.

PETER: (typing slowly, one finger) Ja-son... Theo-dore... Sal... (typing) Sal-zy... How do you spell Salzycowski?

JASON: (helpfully, slowly) Sal-zy-cow-ski.

PETER: (types his best guess. Error sound.) It's -- the field's saying -- (reads screen) -- too many characters.

JASON: (genuinely impressed) Like a video game? Cool!

PETER: No... you know what. Why don't we just leave it as Jason S.

(types. New screen appears.) Okay -- step two. It needs you to set a password.

JASON: Why?

PETER: (reading guide, flatly) "Enhanced security protocols ensure the integrity of each soul's eternal--" (mumbles frustratingly, tapping on screen) Minimum twelve characters. One uppercase. One number. One special character.

(Jason steps up to the tablet and starts typing. Slowly. One finger)

JASON: "Londonbridge".

PETER:  (looking at the screen) That doesn't have a number or a special character.

(Screen starts flashing a loud warning repeatedly.)

JASON: What's happening?? Is it a fire alarm??

PETER: It's saying -- (squinting) -- "please step back from the interface and allow the human user to complete password entry unassisted." (beat. Steps back stiffly.) Take your time.

(Jason types with great focus. Hits enter. Error sound.)

PETER: (stepping back in) You still need the special character.

(Warning flashes again. PLEASE STEP BACK.)

PETER: (steps back. Stiffly.)

(Jason types. Hits enter. Error sound.)

PETER: (through teeth) One more attempt before it locks.

(Jason cracks his knuckles. Takes a breath. Types with enormous concentration)

(Long pause, followed by an error sound)

PETER: (reading screen slowly) Great. It's... locked. For twenty-four hours.

JASON: (sympathetically) I am sorry, I tried.

PETER: That's fine. Trust me, this is not on you. Anyway, let's get this fixed and get you that taco.

(flipping through guide) 

"In the event of account lockout please contact your onboarding supervisor to submit a -- " (flips pages) -- a P-7 reinstatement form through the -- (flips more pages) -- the secondary portal--

JASON: What's the secondary portal?

PETER: (finds page. Reads. Turns guide over. Reads back.) I have no idea. Let me call IT.

(Quick, terse exchange with a colleague offstage. Jason is distracted by the clouds) 

PETER: (puts guide face down) Okay. For now your password is your last name in reverse followed by an @ and a 1.

JASON: (beat) SON-JAYat1? 

PETER: (staring at him) ...Why don't I write that down for you later? (Taps screen) Values alignment survey. Question one. (reading) "In your earthly life, what did you always want but never got?"

JASON: (already staring at something across the room) Yo. Is that an angel?

PETER: Yes! Focus! "In your earthly life, what did you always want but never got?"

JASON: Well I always wanted to take the Uber Boat in Thames, but I don't know swimming so. (already staring at something across the room)

PETER: (very long pause) Why would you have to know... you know what, I am just going to put it down. The system now needs to verify your identity. (reads screen) 

It's sending a code to your mobile number??

JASON: My phone? 

PETER: (befudded) That makes no sense. Let me call up IT again.

(on call with the IT team, away from HeavenOS 2.0) 

AUGUSTUS: (voiceover on phone) It's asking for what? (thinking) Oh I see what's happening. The vendor said the custom implementation is not covered as part of our plan so it's just pulling up from a standard earth onboarding flow.   

(in the background, a notication pops up with HeavenOS 2.0 blaring another warning)

AUGUSTUS: What's that noise?

PETER: Hang on, I'll call you right back.

PETER: No -- no no -- (frantically tapping)

(The welcome jingle plays. Clarence appears on screen.)

HeavenOS 2.0: Inactive User detected. Activating Security protocols. 

Initating transfer to regional partner in 10..9...8...

JASON: (waving cheerfully at the screen) Oh look, Clarence is back!

PETER:  Yay! (sarcastically)

HeavenOS 2.0: 3..2...1 

(Just as Peter turns to say something to Jason, he vanishes)

(Peter calls IT)

PETER: Augustus! The kid just vanished!

AUGUSTUS: Oh yeah, I am fielding calls non-stop from every corner. That's been happening all morning, apparently. it's just a glitch. I am sure he will be back. 

(Peter picks the quick-start guide back up and stares at the screen)

HeavenOS 2.0:  Hi! I'm Clarence! Welcome to HeavenOS 2.0. Let's get you set up for eternity! First--

(Blackout.)

End Tag — Hell

(Red light. Lucifer strolls across, gleeful. Evil demons and minions around, cheering)

LUCIFER: Forty seven! THAT'S FORTY SEVEN MILLION! THIS HOUR ALONE! 

(to a demon nearby) I don't even know what we're doing differently but I'm not going to question it. We are  BACK baby. (spreads arms wide) We are SO back.

(A crowd of bewildered souls mill around looking completely lost. Jason is among them, looking around with gentle curiosity.)

JASON: (to no one in particular) Helloo? Is this Taco Bell?

(Blackout.)
